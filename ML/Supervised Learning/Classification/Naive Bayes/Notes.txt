There are two types of events
1- Independent Variable :: One event dosen't depend on the other one
Eg. rolling of dice 2 times dosen't dependent on each other
2 - Dependent Variable :: One event depend on the other one
Eg. pulling up a card from the deck of cards will change the total numbers of card in the next event.
