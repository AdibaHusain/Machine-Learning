KNN(K NEAREST NEIGHBOUR)
can also be used for continuous var prediction and not only classification
STEPS:
1. Define k value (HYPER PARAMETER)
2. Calculate distance from all points (euclidian distance)
3. sort the distance
4.Majority count (if 3 yes 2 no then output yes)

SUPPOSE IT WAS NOT A CATEGORICAL DATA THEN WE WOULD HAVE FIND OUT THE MEAN VALUE OF THE NEIGHBOURS

FOR K:
must be odd value(same amount of yes no ho sakte hain even me toh decision making would be difficult)
